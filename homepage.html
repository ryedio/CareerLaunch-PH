Program Highlights

Nanay’s in IT Program Batch 5 First Face-to-Face Session
September 15, 2025


A meaningful milestone for Nanay’s in IT Program Batch 5, the first face-to-face session was held on September 15, 2025 at YMCA Manila. It marked the first time the Nanay learners met in person to connect, share stories, and officially begin their IT journey together. The day was filled with learning, inspiration, and a strong sense of community—setting the tone for growth, confidence, and empowerment in tech.

Official Kick-Off of the Nanay’s in IT Program
September 17, 2025


The Nanay’s in IT Program officially kicked off through an online launch that brought together inspiring leaders and partners from Accenture, PBSP, YMCA, and the NIT Program. The event set the vision and direction of the program, highlighting its mission to empower Nanays through digital skills, career opportunities, and inclusive growth. Participants gained insights, encouragement, and a shared sense of purpose as the program formally began its journey of transforming lives through technology.

Nanay’s in IT Program Batch 1 Visit to GreenPod & Sustainability Hub
November 7, 2025


Learners from Nanay’s in IT Program Batch 1 – Team Steph visited GreenPod and the Sustainability Hub to learn about sustainability, innovation, and how technology supports eco-friendly and community-driven solutions.

Nanay’s in IT Program Batch 2 Visit to GreenPod & Sustainability Hub
November 14, 2025


Learners from Nanay’s in IT Program Batch 2 – Team Grace visited GreenPod and the Sustainability Hub to learn about sustainability, innovation, and the role of technology in creating positive environmental impact. The visit inspired the Nanays to connect IT skills with purpose-driven and eco-friendly solutions.

Nanay’s in IT Program Batch 5 Christmas Get-Together
December 15, 2025


The Nanay’s in IT Program Batch 5 came together for a joyful Christmas get-together on December 15, 2025 at YMCA Manila. It was a warm celebration filled with laughter, gratitude, and shared moments, strengthening bonds and celebrating the journey, achievements, and sisterhood of the Nanays in the program.

Nanay’s in IT Program Batch 3 Visit to GreenPod & Sustainability Hub
December 17, 2025


Learners from Nanay’s in IT Program Batch 3 – Team Heizel visited GreenPod and the Sustainability Hub to gain insights on sustainability, innovation, and how technology supports environmentally responsible solutions.

Nanay's in IT Program (NITP)

The Nanay’s in IT Program equips Nanays with essential digital skills to help them access better opportunities and support inclusive growth in the Philippines.

Accenture

Accenture supports the Nanay’s in IT Program by providing expertise, mentorship, and resources that help equip Nanays with in-demand digital skills, empowering them to build sustainable careers in technology.

Philippines Business for Social Progress (PBSP)

PBSP plays a key role in the Nanay’s in IT Program by bringing together partners and resources to support skills training, community development, and inclusive opportunities for Nanays in the Philippines.

Young Men's Christian Association (YMCA)

YMCA supports the Nanay’s in IT Program by providing a safe learning space, community support, and programs that help Nanays grow in skills, confidence, and opportunities for a better future.
